Brown Butter & Sea Salt Cookies

Description:

Delicious shortbread cookies with a nutty, buttery flavor and a hint of salty goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp bourbon
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • coarse sea salt

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325*
  2. Heat unsalted butter in small saucepan over medium low heat until the milk solids brown. Watch closely so it does not burn.
  3. Move browned butter to a mixing bowl.
  4. Mix in brown sugar, vanilla, and bourbon.
  5. In a separate bowl, gently whisk together flour and baking soda.
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the butter & sugar mixture and stir until comlpetely incorporated.
  7. If the dough is too dry, add a small amount of water.
  8. Shape into 1-inch balls and place 2 inches apart on a silicone baking mat. Press down lightly to shape into flat rounds.
  9. Spinkle each cookie with coarse sea salt.
  10. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes.
  11. Allow to cool completely before serving.
